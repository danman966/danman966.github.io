About

This is the website which was formerly home to
  • DanMat computer shop (Sutton Coldfield, Birmingham),
  • DanMat computer services (Devon), and
  • Brassware Dealer (Online)
Both businesses have unfortunately been closed.

The links on the left will take you to the business pages at different times in its lifecycle. These have been recovered and remastered from the Internet Archive.

Trivia

DanMat was named after Terry's two sons, Daniel and Matthew (the former of which is writing this).

Terry would never charge anyone unless he actually fixed their computer problems.

Terry would often give discounts if he knew someone was struggling financially, or having other difficulties.

Me and my brother would often tag along to jobs, and Terry would give us opportunities to help out (and would pay us more than we deserved).
